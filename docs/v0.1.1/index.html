Our Approach

Structured, Grade-Specific STEM Programs

Each program is built around the specific academic demands of its grade band — not adapted from a generic template. Students receive instructor-guided support at every stage of development.

01

Grade-Specific Curriculum

Programs are designed for each grade band, not adapted from a one-size-fits-all template. Content aligns with academic expectations at each level of development.

02

Instructor-Guided Sessions

Students receive structured academic support in focused sessions designed to reinforce classroom learning and address individual skill gaps directly.

03

STEM-Centered Instruction

Mathematics and science are integrated across every program to connect academic skills to real-world reasoning and future STEM pathways.

04

Progress-Based Advancement

Students advance through structured skill progressions with clear milestones, giving families a measurable view of academic development over time.

Elementary School

Grades 3–5 Program Details

Grade 3

Foundations & Exploration

Students build foundational academic skills through structured exercises in core subjects, with guided introduction to scientific observation and mathematical reasoning.

  • Mathematics: Number sense, basic operations, introduction to patterns
  • Science: Observation skills, life science basics, scientific method introduction
  • Reading & Writing: Comprehension strategies, structured sentence writing
Grade 4

Core Skill Development

Students strengthen multi-step reasoning and written communication while exploring earth science concepts and building fluency in core mathematics.

  • Mathematics: Multi-digit operations, fraction concepts, geometry basics
  • Science: Earth science, matter and energy fundamentals
  • Reading & Writing: Informational text analysis, research skills introduction
Grade 5

Applied Learning & Reasoning

Students apply mathematical and scientific thinking to structured problems, developing the reasoning skills needed for middle school academic transition.

  • Mathematics: Fractions, decimals, introduction to pre-algebraic thinking
  • Science: Physical science, ecosystems, data collection and recording
  • Reading & Writing: Analytical reading, structured argument writing
Middle School

Grades 6–8 Program Details

Grade 6

Transition & Analytical Thinking

Students develop analytical reasoning and academic independence during a critical transition period, with structured support across core STEM and literacy skills.

  • Mathematics: Ratios, proportions, early algebra concepts
  • Science: Life science, cells, introduction to scientific investigation
  • Reading & Writing: Evidence-based analysis, academic vocabulary development
Grade 7

STEM Concepts & Study Skills

Students strengthen problem-solving skills and academic discipline through focused algebra instruction and applied science concepts, with structured study habits built in throughout.

  • Mathematics: Algebra I foundations, geometry, statistics introduction
  • Science: Chemistry and physics concepts, scientific reasoning
  • Reading & Writing: Argumentative writing, research and citation skills
Grade 8

Pre-High School Preparation

Students consolidate middle school learning and build the academic readiness required for high school coursework, with emphasis on algebra fluency, data interpretation, and written analysis.

  • Mathematics: Linear equations, functions, Algebra I
  • Science: Physical science, engineering concepts, data interpretation
  • Reading & Writing: Academic writing, synthesis and textual analysis
High School

Grades 9–12 Program Details

Grade 9

High School Foundations

Students establish the academic habits and content knowledge required for high school success, with structured support in algebra, laboratory science, and academic writing.

  • Mathematics: Algebra I and II support, functions, coordinate geometry
  • Science: Biology, chemistry introduction, lab methodology
  • Academic Skills: Study planning, academic writing, critical reading
Grade 10

Core STEM Advancement

Students deepen STEM knowledge across geometry, chemistry, and analytical reasoning, building toward advanced coursework and developing independent academic problem-solving skills.

  • Mathematics: Geometry, trigonometry basics, Algebra II
  • Science: Chemistry, earth and environmental science
  • Academic Skills: Research methodology, advanced coursework preparation
Grade 11

Advanced Subject Preparation

Students prepare for advanced and college-level coursework through focused instruction in pre-calculus, physics, and structured academic writing, with support for standardized test readiness.

  • Mathematics: Pre-calculus, statistics, advanced algebra
  • Science: Physics, advanced biology or chemistry
  • Academic Skills: Standardized test preparation, college-level writing
Grade 12

College & Career Readiness

Students finalize their academic preparation with instruction in calculus, statistics, and advanced sciences alongside targeted support for college applications, placement assessments, and academic writing.

  • Mathematics: Calculus introduction, statistics, college math placement
  • Science: Advanced STEM electives, research project skills
  • Academic Skills: College application support, advanced academic writing
